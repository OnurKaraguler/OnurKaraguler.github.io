About Me

Transition to Data Science ...

I am a mechanical engineer who has switched my career to Data Science. While working, I completed my Master of Business Administration (MBA-English) in 2012. I started my PhD in Business Administration in 2013. Statistics lessons I took during my academic education made me interested in data science. With this passion, I have developed myself as a professional Data Scientist. I can do data related jobs including Data Science, Data Analysis, Machine learning, Data Mining, Data Preprocessing, Data Visualization, Web Scraping. I have solid programming experience in Python and I can build a desktop applications for windows, linux and Mac using PyQt5 package.
